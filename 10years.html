10 year prototype

Brief - "Design a game in 1 night, that you can prototype in 1 week, that you can play for 10 years."

Cube Infinity
Get circles! Three in a row increases your size and multiplier. Avoid triangles! Three will take your multiplier and 25 points.
Highway Chase
Speed down the highway to catch the escaping villain in your Austin martin db5. !!Explosions!!
Cannon Kings
Build a castle to protect your king from the devastating fury of the enemies cannon fire. Hit the enemy king with a cannon ball to win.
GooGoo
Make your way through this level and collect Goo to make yourself invincible.
Loot-To-Boot
You've found a Chest that contains an infinite amount of loot. What are you going to do? Break it open of course!
Oh Shit! Aliens!
Oh Shit! Aliens! was a prototype made under the theme "A game you could play for ten years." This game was the first game made this trimester and was made in one week in c++.
