Description

Get Kraken is a 2D web game in which the player must collect golden fish and release the kraken while holding off a horde of annoying tourists. The player must catch fish which can be used as currency to upgrade their fishing abilities or purchase traps to keep tourists away.

The player controls the ghost of an old fisherman that is trying to keep the lake peaceful and quiet so the living fishermen can enjoy themselves.

While in Fishing mode, the ghost controls the fishing line of the old man and manipulates it to maximize the number of fish that are caught.

While in Tourist Defence mode, the ghost can move around the path and scare tourists away as well as spend fish to place scary traps.
